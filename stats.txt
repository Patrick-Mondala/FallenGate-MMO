Primary stats:

- Strength: melee (crushing, slashing, and piercing) damage and base armor.

classes: Warrior, Paladin, Shadowknight.

- Agility: atkspeed/movementspeed & evasion (dodge + block chance)

classes: Rogue.

- Intelligence: Spell damage, how fast magic spells cast, resists

classes: Wizard, Warlock.

- Dexterity: crit chance/proc chance, concentration, melee skillups

classes: Ranger

- Spirit: mana regen per sec, healing % increased. 
classes: Shaman, Druid.

- Wisdom: increases mana by 2 points, lower mana cost per spell, fizzle chance lowered
classes: Cleric
